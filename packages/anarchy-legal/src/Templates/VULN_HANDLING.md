- Contact: security@<domain>, response in 3 business days.
- Triage → fix → release security update.

  - t+24h: initial notice to national CSIRT (and ENISA for serious incidents).
  - t+72h: follow-up report.
  - ≤14 days after mitigation: final report; users receive advisory/release notes.
