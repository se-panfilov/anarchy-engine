# Security Support Period Policy (CRA)

**Product:** TBD until market release (the “Software”)
**Start of support:** TBD until market release (first commercial release in the EU)

**Security support period (per major version):** the **shorter** of:

- **24 months** (declared expected lifetime) **from the initial commercial release** of that major version, **or**
- **five (5) years** from that initial commercial release.

**Notes**

- Updates are delivered via distribution channels used for the Software; no fixed cadence/SLA is guaranteed.
- Security fixes are provided **free of charge** during the support period. Functionality/content updates are described in release notes (if any).
